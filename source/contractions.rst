Contractions
============
In general, contractions are created by folding in a short suffix.
We include `*` as well to remind us of the apostrophe.

Personal Pronouns
-----------------
"To be" contractions.
Eg, "am", "are", "is".
Also includes "has".

* :steno:`I'm <*EUPL>`
* :steno:`you're <*UR>`
* :steno:`he's <H*ES>`
* :steno:`she's <SH*ES>`
* :steno:`it's <*TS>`
* :steno:`we're <W*ER>`
* :steno:`they're <TH*ER>`

"have" contractions.

* :steno:`I've <*EUF>`
* :steno:`you've <*UF>`
* :steno:`we've <W*EF>`
* :steno:`they've <TH*EF>`

"would" and "had" contractions.

* :steno:`I'd <*EUD>`
* :steno:`you'd <*UD>`
* :steno:`he'd <H*ED>`
* :steno:`she'd <SH*ED>`
* :steno:`it'd <*TD>`
* :steno:`we'd <W*ED>`
* :steno:`they'd <TH*ED>`

* :steno:`won't <W*PB>`
* :steno:`isn't <S*PB>`
